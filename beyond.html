Beyond – A Shared Wellness Experience

An intimate app concept that brings couples together around a plant-based lifestyle.

UX/UI Lifestyle Mobile App

Project Overview

Beyond is a conceptual app designed to elevate the shared experience of wellness through a premium plant-based drink. Created for couples who prioritize conscious living, the app invites users to discover, taste, and connect around a lifestyle of intentional choices.

💡 Brief

Design an engaging mobile experience to promote a high-end vegetarian beverage for couples, encouraging shared moments and mindful rituals.

🌱 Concept

Beyond blends e-commerce with lifestyle — combining smooth shopping, daily rituals, and curated pair experiences that foster connection and wellness.

🎨 Visual Direction

A calming palette rooted in soft greens and neutrals reflects the organic and serene nature of the product. Rounded elements and fluid transitions support a peaceful interaction.

💑 Audience

Modern couples seeking balance, health, and meaningful connection — both with each other and with what they consume.
